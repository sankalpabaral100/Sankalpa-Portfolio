About Pokhara Research Centre

PRC is a non-partisan organisation based in Kaski District, Gandaki Province. It focuses on economic policy reforms, governance, and engaging the community in the policymaking process. Primary clients include young thinkers, students, intellectuals, and policymakers. PRC's vision is to strengthen individual liberty and enhance economic equity through a well-informed electorate.
