About Me

I'm Sankalpa Baral, an Associate Program Manager at Pokhara Research Centre — a non-partisan think tank in Pokhara, Nepal. My work sits at the intersection of public policy, community development, and program strategy, translating research into meaningful civic action across Gandaki Province.

Before moving into the policy space, I built a strong operational foundation in hospitality management at Hotel Monal, progressing from Front Office Allrounder to Assistant Hotel Manager over five years. I also bring hands-on banking experience from Rastriya Banijya Bank and ongoing regional program coordination with National Banking Institute.

"Adaptability, hard work, and a genuine commitment to people — these are the values I carry into every role. Effective coordination is 20% strategy and 80% consistent follow-through."

Beyond work, I'm passionate about voice-over and narration, travelling, and community volunteering — pursuits that sharpen communication skills and deepen connection to the communities I serve.
